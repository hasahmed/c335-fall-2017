Final Project Description


Journey of the Prary King

Game Description:
    You play as The Prary King, a Cowboy who is being barraded by bandits. Your goal is to not die as wave after wave of bandit
    comes to kill you. You may find a power up here and there to make your gun or player better in various ways

Techical Details:
    SD card to save high score
    Nunchuk1 to control players movement, and nunchuk2 to control direction of players gun
    Maybe: read images from SD card for static background.
